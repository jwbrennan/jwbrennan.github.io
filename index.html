About Me

For the past 18 months I have worked for the technical consulting department of Wolfram Research. I help provide solutions to clients using Wolfram technologies.

My background in astrophysics allows me to effectively solve complex problems and deliver high-quality results. I also deliver training and webinars showcasing the capabilities of Wolfram products.
